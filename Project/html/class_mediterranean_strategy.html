Detailed Description

Implements care strategy for Mediterranean climate plants.

This strategy is designed for plants that thrive in Mediterranean climates characterized by hot, dry summers and mild, wet winters. These plants require moderate watering and balanced fertilization, adapting to seasonal variations.

Typical plants using this strategy include lavender, rosemary, olive trees, and other herbs and shrubs native to Mediterranean regions.
