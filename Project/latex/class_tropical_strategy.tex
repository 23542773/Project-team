\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implements care strategy for tropical climate plants. 

This strategy is designed for plants that thrive in warm, humid tropical environments. Tropical plants require frequent watering and regular fertilization to support their vigorous growth in high-\/nutrient, moist conditions.

Typical plants using this strategy include ferns, orchids, bromeliads, and other moisture-\/loving tropical species.
