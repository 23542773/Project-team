\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implements care strategy for wetland/bog plants. 

This strategy is designed for plants that thrive in wetland environments with consistently high moisture levels. Wetland plants require very frequent watering to maintain saturated soil conditions and moderate fertilization to support growth in nutrient-\/rich aquatic environments.

Typical plants using this strategy include water lilies, cattails, sedges, and other aquatic or semi-\/aquatic species.
