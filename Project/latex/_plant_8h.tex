\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Declares the \doxylink{class_plant}{Plant} class, representing an individual plant instance within the greenhouse system. 

The \doxylink{class_plant}{Plant} class composes multiple pattern-\/based components including\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}\+Flyweight (\doxylink{class_plant_flyweight}{Plant\+Flyweight})\+: Shares intrinsic species data.
\item \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}\+Strategy (\doxylink{class_care_strategy}{Care\+Strategy})\+: Encapsulates biome-\/specific care behavior (e.\+g., watering, fertilizing).
\item \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}\+State (\doxylink{class_plant_state}{Plant\+State})\+: Tracks and transitions between growth stages.
\item \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}\+Prototype\+: Enables efficient cloning of existing plants.
\end{DoxyItemize}

Each plant maintains its own mutable attributes such as moisture, health, and insecticide levels, while delegating fixed species data to shared flyweights. Time-\/based simulation enables plants to age dynamically and respond to environmental care routines.
