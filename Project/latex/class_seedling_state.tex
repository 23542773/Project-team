\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Concrete State class representing a young plant in its early growth stage. 

This state represents the initial viable growth stage of a plant after germination. Seedlings have moderate resource consumption and are vulnerable to poor conditions. Can transition to \doxylink{class_growing_state}{Growing\+State} when age and health thresholds are met, or to \doxylink{class_dead_state}{Dead\+State} if health drops to zero.
